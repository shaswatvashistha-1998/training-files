Realme x3

Realme X3 smartphone was launched on 25th June 2020. The phone comes with a 6.60-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2400 pixels and an aspect ratio of 20:9. Realme X3 is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 855+ processor. It comes with 6GB of RAM. The Realme X3 runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4200mAh non-removable battery. The Realme X3 supports proprietary fast charging.
